Objectives

IWeDA 2021 The Next Level will showcase cutting-edge digital architecture and anticipate the coming new world of digital-based architecture.

Digital Architecture (DA) is not about drawing a building using a computer. It is about exploring, utilizing, embedding information technology products into the architecture world. The role of digital technology in architecture design started, as most people believe, when we applied computer-aided architecture design. Since all the design processes are happening in our brain, a computer is just a tool. A computer is just our added-hands. Now, we see digital technology is advancing beyond our imagination. The development of artificial intelligence (and artificial emotion) offers new ways of using computers. Digital computation comprehensively involves in the architecture design process. A laptop becomes our added-brain. The webinar expects around 150 participants, including professional architects, academics, students, and public.

Topics

DA is advancing thanks to the new crazy ideas and sophisticated software and hardware development. Innovation in DA will create a future un-precedented architecture world. This International Webinar on Digital Architecture (IWeDA) challenges anyone to share their brightest ideas on embedding digital technology into the architecture world.
