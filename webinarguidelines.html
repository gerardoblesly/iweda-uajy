HOUSE RULES

We would like to thank you for participating in the International Webinar on Digital Architecture 2021 (IWeDA 2021). Here is the information for joining IWeDA Online Speakers Session which will be held online on December 13th-14th via Zoom Cloud Meeting.

We would really appreciate it if you are willing to pay attention to some “House Rules” for IWeDA 2021:

  1. Host will start Zoom meeting early for the Webinar registration.
  2. To ensure the security of the session, we will need you to log in using your email address. Please be sure that your name is written correctly and using the format “P_Name_Institution”.
  3. Please keep your microphone on mute and turn off your video during the session to allow smooth bandwidth during the session.
  4. During the panel discussions, be sure to join the Zoom meeting that you have registered.
  5. Immediately after the completion of the panel discussions, please return to the plenary room.
  6. Link to the video meetings will be provided approaching the event dates.
